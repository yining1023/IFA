Consortia

Contemporary Art Consortium
The Contemporary Art Consortium at New York University’s Institute of Fine Arts acts as a network and outlet for modern and contemporary students and alumni of the IFA. Our mission is to connect our academic community with artists, scholars, curators, conservators, gallerists, collectors and other New Yorkers with an interest in the arts. Click here to read more.

New York Renaissance Consortium
The New York Renaissance Consortium's purpose is to bring together scholars, students, curators, and others with interest in the arts of the Renaissance.  The Consortium is a network for information on research and activities in the New York area. It will hold a number of Consortium events each season, such as panels and discussions. Click here to read more.

The Arts of China Consortium
The ACC was founded in 2002 to promote the study and understanding of Chinese art history, archaeology, and visual and material culture, and to support the research and professional activities of its members. To encourage more informal dialogue among members, ACC conducts an e-mail listserv and maintains this website to provide information on graduate programs in Asian art, lectures and conferences, grants and fellowships, positions available, and web links to facilitate research and pedagogy. Click here to read more.
