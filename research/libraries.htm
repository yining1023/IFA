Libraries

Welcome to the Libraries website, containing information about our network of libraries and services, as well as access to online research tools.

The Institute maintains both the Stephen Chan Library at the Duke House and the Conservation Center Library at the Conservation Center, as well as full access to Bobst Library, NYU's main library. In addition, the Institute is part of a larger consortium of libraries at other Institutions.
