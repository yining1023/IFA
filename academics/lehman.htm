Robert Lehman Collection Scholarly Catalogue Project

The Institute of Fine Arts, in a joint venture with the Robert Lehman Foundation and The Metropolitan Museum of Art, is engaged in the preparation for publication of a comprehensive scholarly catalogue of the Robert Lehman Collection in The Metropolitan Museum of Art. Outstanding specialists participate, and advanced Institute students are selected to serve as research assistants to the project. The Director of the Institute, Professor Patricia Rubin, serves as Project Executive; Professor Egbert Haverkamp-Begemann is Coordinating Scholar.
