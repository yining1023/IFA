Entertain at the James B. Duke House

Entertaining at the Duke House is an exclusive benefit reserved for our dedicated members. Whether it is an intimate dinner party in our handsome Loeb Room, a large-scale event in the Great Hall, or a meeting in our Seminar Room, the Duke House provides a unique setting for a wide variety of special occasions. This historic landmark has retained all of the charm and elegance from its inception over a century ago. With many of its original walls, floors, ceilings and fixtures, your event is sure to reflect the character and atmosphere of the renowned Duke mansion.
