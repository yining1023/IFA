The IFA Recent Alumni Circle

The Recent Alumni Circle recognizes IFA graduates from the last fifteen years who make an annual gift to their alma mater in support of the students following in their footsteps. With a modest annual gift to the Institute, alumni can join the Recent Alumni Circle and receive invitations to events designed just for them, such as cocktails at a gallery or a curator-led exhibition tour. Events are hosted by recent alumni and serve to highlight their work, while providing opportunities for networking, socializing and building relationships with fellow IFA alum. 

The Recent Alumni Circle pays tribute to the great work that recent alumni are doing within their professional fields while providing philanthropic support to the next generation of scholars. Members to the Recent Alumni Circle make a gift to the IFA at the following levels:

   Graduates from the last 0-5 years: $75

   Graduates from the last 5-10 years: $150

   Graduates from the last 10-15 years: $250

To make your gift and become a member of the IFA Recent Alumni Circle, please click here.

If you would like to host an event for the Recent Alumni Circle, please email Andrea Yglesias at andrea.yglesias@nyu.edu.

Members of the Recent Alumni Circle Committee make an annual gift of $1,000 or more to the Institute, provide insight on ideas for potential events and act as ambassadors for the school.
