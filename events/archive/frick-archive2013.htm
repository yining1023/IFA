The IFA - Frick Symposium

For more than half a century, The Frick Collection and the Institute of Fine Arts have hosted a symposium for graduate students in art history. Students at all stages of their degree program are encouraged to submit
papers that demonstrate the breadth and depth of research carried out at their institution. The symposium offers candidates the opportunity to deliver original research papers in a public forum and to engage with colleagues in the field — novice and expert.

The 2014 IFA - Frick Symposium will be held on April 25 and April 26, 2014 at the Institute of Fine Arts and the Frick Collection in New York City. Please check this website for updates.

The In-HouseIFA - Frick Symposium will be held on January 31st, 2014 at the Institute of Fine Arts.
