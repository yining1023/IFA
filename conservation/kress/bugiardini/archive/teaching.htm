Teaching from the Kress Collection

The teaching material below is provided to give students a greater understanding of paintings by incorporating conservation, science and technical art history into lesson-plans and curricula. Downloadable educational resources are provided for teachers and other education specialists to be used in class instruction, in hardcopy handouts or in Powerpoint presentations.
